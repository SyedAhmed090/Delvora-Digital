Last updated: 30 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Delvora Digital Studio ("Delvora", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website or contact us. By using this site, you agree to the practices described here.

We only collect information you choose to give us, plus basic usage data:

  • Usage & analytics data — like most websites, we use analytics tools that collect anonymised information such as pages visited, time on site, device and browser type, and approximate location (derived from IP address). This is collected via cookies and similar technologies.

2. How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiry and provide a quote or proposal.

3. Cookies & analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand site traffic. Google Analytics sets cookies that help us measure how visitors interact with the site. This data is aggregated and not used to personally identify you. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, or by disabling cookies in your browser settings.

4. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only with trusted service providers who help us operate (for example, our analytics provider, web hosting, and email delivery), and only to the extent needed to provide those services. We may also disclose information if required by law.

5. Data retention

We keep enquiry information for as long as needed to respond to you and for our legitimate business records, after which it is deleted or anonymised.

6. Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. To make a request, email us at hello@delvoradigital.com and we'll respond within a reasonable time.

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Our site may link to external websites we don't control. This policy doesn't cover their practices — please review their privacy policies separately.
